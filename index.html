Bass OS

Android 12 (Android 14 coming soon)

x86_64-v2/v3 PC's, Laptops, SBC's, Tablets & 2-in-1's 

Bass OS (Broad Apparatus Support System) is a highly configurable open-source toolkit and OS based on Android-x86 and Bliss OS, and it works with most Windows, ChromeOS & Linux PC hardware.

Bass OS contains only proprietary drivers, firmware and codecs, and is Google free by default, but can also come with MicroG included for applications that rely on Google Play Services.

Kiosk Launcher

Simple Kiosk mode launcher with app auto-launch support

ARM/ARM64/x86/x86_64

Kiosk Launcher allows you to configure minimal options and a set of whitelisted apps for you to allow your users access to.

It also supports setting an app to autolaunch on boot and locks the app to the screen.

Our Kiosk Launcher is only available with our Bass OS builds, or with Bass Toolkit adapted to your BSP. 

  • Kiosk Launcher (uses system policies)
  • Lockdown/Admin Modes
  • Auto-Launch app
  • Singlei-Display Support
